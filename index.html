Description

This is a collection of historical Michigan gubernatorial executive orders from 1955-1999. This static website does not search for subject matter or text inside the documents. It is for quick lookups by users with executive order numbers in hand or who wish to browse by year

How to Search this Collection

This website is useful to researchers who already have an executive order number or want to browse years.

To find a specific executive order, enter the numeric designation and year into the search box above. Alternatively, click on the Timeline button above and browse by year.

This website does not search the text of any documents. It only looks for years and executive order numeric designations.
